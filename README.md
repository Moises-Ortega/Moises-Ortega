# Hi there! 👋 I'm a Computer Engineer from UNAM

I'm currently in my 8th semester of Computer Engineering at the Facultad de Ingeniería (UNAM). I am deeply passionate about the evolution of technology, with a strong focus on operating systems, open-source philosophy, sysadmin, and network infrastructure.

When I'm not configuring my Arch Linux, you can find me following the competitive e-sports scene of Valorant, enjoying the tranquility of nature on a long walk, or discovering new music.

### 🚀 What I do & What I'm passionate about
* **Infrastructure & Networking:** Building and managing environments using Docker, Nginx, Cloudflare, and Tailscale. I have completed training for the RHCSA and I'm currently actively studying for CCNA.
* **Software Engineering & Security:** Developing robust backend applications. I enjoy building secure systems, such as implementing document validation systems utilizing cryptographic signatures.
* **Algorithmic Problem Solving:** Actively sharpening my analytical skills through competitive programming and tackling complex algorithmic challenges on platforms like LeetCode.
* **Hardware & Architecture:** Deep understanding of low-level processes, Assembly language, VLSI, and computer graphics.
